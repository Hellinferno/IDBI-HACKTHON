# WealthOrb — Demo Run-of-Show (≈3 minutes)

**Persona:** Rahul Sharma, a salaried IDBI customer. ₹2,00,000 in savings, a ₹15,000/month
SIP, ₹3,50,000 invested so far. Like most mass-market customers, he has *never* had a
personal wealth advisor. One demo user shows three different problems WealthOrb solves.

**Setup:** open the deployed link. The orb idles (calm blue). Have these 4 questions ready
as suggestion chips so nothing is typed live.

---

## The script

### 0. One-line opener (say this before touching anything)
> "IDBI has millions of customers, but real financial advice is reserved for the wealthy
> few. WealthOrb is a voice-first AI advisor that gives *every* customer guidance they can
> trust — and crucially, an AI that **cannot invent a number**. Let me show you."

### 1. Affordability  → the hook
**Ask:** "Can I afford a 20 lakh car in 3 years?"
- Orb shifts **listening → thinking → speaking**; a Goal card animates in.
- **Point at the card:** "Every figure here — the ₹23.8L required, the ₹12.6L gap — was
  computed by our deterministic engine. The AI only phrased it. See the *Engine-verified*
  badge and *How this was calculated*."
- **The money line:** "The model literally never touches the math. That's what makes AI
  safe to ship inside a bank."

### 2. Idle cash  → cross-sell / deposit story
**Ask:** "Do I have idle cash to invest?"
- **Point:** "It found ₹50,000 sitting idle after protecting a 3-month emergency buffer,
  and suggests a liquid fund. That's engagement *and* a product cross-sell — money that
  otherwise leaks to a fintech app stays in the IDBI ecosystem."

### 3. Tax  → relevance / timing
**Ask:** "Help me save tax under 80C"
- **Point:** "₹60,000 of his 80C limit is unused. Timely, personal, actionable — the kind
  of nudge a relationship manager would give, at zero marginal cost, for every customer."

### 4. Free-form  → prove it's not scripted
**Ask (type something off-script):** "Why is investing early better than waiting?"
- **Point:** "No engine number for this, so it answers conceptually — and notice it refuses
  to invent any figure. Ask it anything; it stays grounded."

### Close (10 seconds)
> "A 3D voice advisor every customer remembers, that can't give an unsuitable or fabricated
> answer — deeper engagement, more deposits retained, more products placed. That's
> WealthOrb."

---

## Talking points to have in your pocket
- **Trust moat:** numbers come only from the engine (96 deterministic tests, exact to 2
  decimals); recommendations pass a suitability gate; every shown rec is auditable.
- **Why a bank cares:** retention of idle deposits, MF/insurance/ELSS cross-sell, advisory
  at scale with no human cost, regulatory-defensible AI.
- **Built but not shown today (mention, don't demo):** Hindi support, voice STT/TTS,
  rebalancing, hash-chained audit log, consent enforcement.

## Safety net
- If the live link or Gemini stalls, the orchestrator auto-falls back to deterministic
  phrasing — the demo still runs. Keep a **recorded screen capture** of this exact script
  as backup.
