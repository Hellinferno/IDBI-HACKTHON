# 01 — Product Vision

## Vision

Give every bank customer a personal wealth advisor that fits in their pocket, knows their money, and tells the truth — at the scale of millions, at near-zero marginal cost.

## Target users

| Segment | Need | Current gap |
|---|---|---|
| Mass-market salaried | "Am I saving enough? Where do I put idle money?" | No advisor; only generic product pushes |
| Mass-affluent | Goal planning, tax optimization, rebalancing | Advisor too expensive / impersonal |
| First-time investors | Guidance in plain language, vernacular | Jargon, fear, no starting point |
| Self-directed | Sanity-check, what-if simulation | No personalized data-backed feedback |

## Product principles

1. **Truthful over impressive** — never a number the engine cannot defend.
2. **Proactive over reactive** — surface what matters before the user asks.
3. **Calm over flashy** — the Orb reassures; finance is anxiety-prone.
4. **Embedded over standalone** — lives inside the bank app the user already trusts.
5. **Explainable** — every suggestion can be expanded into "why".

## What the Orb does (user-facing)

- Greets and summarizes financial health in one line.
- Answers spoken or typed questions, grounded in the user's own data + bank catalog.
- Proposes goals and shows live corpus/gap with a what-if slider.
- Nudges on idle cash, tax gaps, missed SIPs, overspend, goal drift.
- Reacts visually: calm blue when healthy, amber on attention items, warm pulse on celebration.

## Success metrics

| Metric | Target (post-launch) |
|---|---|
| Advisory sessions / monthly active user | ≥ 4 |
| Recommendation acceptance rate | ≥ 15% |
| Idle-cash mobilized | tracked ₹ value |
| Goal-plan creation rate | ≥ 25% of engaged users |
| Suitability violations reaching user | **0** |
| Avg. advisory response latency | < 2.5s first token |

## Non-goals (v1)

- Executing trades/transactions automatically without explicit consent.
- Replacing human RM for HNI/private banking tier.
- Tax filing.
- Lending/credit advisory.
